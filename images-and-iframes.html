About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, born in Madeira, Portugal in 1985,
rose from humble beginnings to become one of the greatest footballers of
all time. Known for his speed, skill, and relentless drive, he began his
professional career with Sporting CP before making a name at
Manchester United. His legendary stint at Real Madrid saw him break
countless records and win multiple Champions League titles.
Later, he played for Juventus, returned to Manchester United, and
currently stars for Al-Nassr. A five-time Ballon d’Or winner and Portugal’s
all-time top scorer, Ronaldo’s legacy is defined by his unmatched work ethic,
influence, and love for the beautiful game.


Ronaldo's Celebrations

The “Siuuu” celebration started around 2013 when Ronaldo played for Real Madrid. During a game in the U.S.,

he shouted “Sii!” (Spanish for “Yes!”) after scoring, and fans loved it. He kept doing it,

adding the spin and jump later. Since then, it’s become his signature move and a symbol of his success.

Ronaldo' Ionic move

The Ronaldo Chop is one of Cristiano Ronaldo’s signature moves. He uses it to quickly change direction and

trick defenders. To do it, he jumps slightly, swings one leg across his body, and cuts the ball behind his other

leg. It’s fast, flashy, and great for beating opponents, messi is better especially when running down the wing.
